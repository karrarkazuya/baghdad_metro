Metro Guides

Why Metro

We believe people should start using metro instead of cars and here are our top 4 reasons why

1

Fast and Efficient

Metro systems are designed to transport people quickly and efficiently through cities. With dedicated tracks and high-speed trains, you can avoid traffic congestion and arrive at your destination faster than driving or taking a bus.

2

Cost-Effective

Using the metro can be a cost-effective alternative to other modes of transportation. With a variety of fare options and discounts available, you can save money on your daily commute or when traveling around the city.

3

Environmentally Friendly

Metro systems are more environmentally friendly than cars or buses, as they produce fewer greenhouse gas emissions and consume less energy per passenger. By using the metro, you can help reduce air pollution and contribute to a cleaner, healthier city.

4

Safe and Secure

Metro systems typically have dedicated security personnel and surveillance systems to ensure the safety of passengers. With well-lit stations, clear signage, and easy-to-use ticketing systems, you can feel confident and secure while traveling on the metro.

TICKETS

With online ticketing, you can avoid the crowds and start your metro journey smoothly and conveniently.

Single Ride Tickets

0.5 IQD

  1. Perfect for occasional or one-time riders.
  2. No expiration date.
  3. Cheaper than buying multiple rides.
  4. Can be easily shared with others.

Daily Pass

2 IQD

  1. Unlimited rides for 24 hours from the time of purchase.
  2. Cost-effective for a full day of metro travel.
  3. Easy to use with no need to purchase multiple tickets.
  4. Ideal for tourists or visitors exploring the city.
  5. Saves time compared to purchasing tickets for each ride.

Monthly Pass

30 IQD

  1. Provides unlimited rides for a month.
  2. Saves money compared to purchasing individual tickets daily.
  3. Convenient and eliminates the need to purchase tickets daily.
  4. Ideal for daily commuters who use the metro regularly.
  5. Can be easily renewed online or at ticket vending machines.
